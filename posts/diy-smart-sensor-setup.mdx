## Build It Yourself

Creating your own smart sensor can save money and give you full control. This guide walks you through:

- Sourcing parts like the ESP32 and LD2410
- Wiring and testing
- Adding YAML to Home Assistant
- Mounting it cleanly in your home

Great for hobbyists or pros wanting deeper control.
